THE OBEROI / DUBAI / UAE

Sustainable design, resilient transportation and empowered youth - the keys to a brighter future

1 June 2023

Main Program

Moderator's welcome introduction

The impact that architecture has had on the development of human society, and how the latest trends in lifestyle preferences are driving a new generation of architects and localised design values.

Why the Future City is a gym

The new-style cities of the future will involve a series of clusters, where all needs - from work to leisure to wellness - can be satisfactorily met.

The power of re-purposing

Demolition and massive re-building enterprises is costly, it makes strong economic and social sense to re-purpose buildings - creating fresh and vibrant communities around local architectural and community hubs.

Awards presentation

Six prestigious awards will be given to the very best of tomorrow's Architects - fresh graduates from one of the UAE's most demanding and prestigious faculties.

Moderator's closing remarks

Moderator closes the day event summarising the take a way points and thanking all the speakers, delegates, sponsors and partners.
